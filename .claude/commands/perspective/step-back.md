What are we trying to achieve? Why? How are we going about it?

Is that approach the simplest way we can do it? Could we make any of this simpler? Reflect on this.

Does everything we have done fully align with the rules and guides linked to from .agent/directives-and-memory/AGENT.md?

What do type-check and lint say?

Are the tests passing?

Evaluate where we are, then take a step back and think hard about where we want to be, and the cleanest way to get there.

Then reflect on all of that, and decide what to do next. We don't have to do it quickly, but we do need to thoughtfully build our foundations.
