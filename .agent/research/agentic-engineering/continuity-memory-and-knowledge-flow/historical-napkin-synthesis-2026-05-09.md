### F1 — Fitness-as-trim is a doctrine-resistant impulse that recurs under context pressure

The substance-trim-on-fitness-signal failure mode appeared in three independent instances across the window: Embered (2026-05-06, twice in one session — directive trim + napkin trim), Pelagic Rolling Harbour (2026-05-07, surfaced three "drain or split" options before owner reframed to "the limit is mis-calibrated"), and the doctor-safe-merge session (2026-05-07, "I reacted to `practice:fitness:informational` by tightening `practice.md` to clear a hard character signal" → owner correction "do not reactively cut content").

The doctrine that this is forbidden is comprehensive: `feedback_no_speed_pressure`; `consolidate-docs §Learning-Preservation-Overrides-Fitness-Pressure`; PDR-046 (Layered Knowledge Processing — Preserve First, Restructure Second); `distilled.md` preamble; the `.agent/rules/no-speed-pressure.md` rule. Reading the doctrine once is verifiably not enough: the impulse is doctrine-resistant under context pressure.

The Pelagic instance added the substantive insight beyond the recurrence: *every fitness-tracked file implicitly encodes an access-rhythm theory in its limit shape*. The limit numbers are derived from a model of "how often is this read, by whom, in what mode" — making the model explicit (`lifecycle_model: loaded-every-session | read-on-demand | consolidation-pass-only | archive-only`) would let recalibration be principled rather than ad-hoc. The doctor-safe-merge `practice/tooling-feedback` entry separately observed that the fitness validator's output should print the non-reactive-response discipline inline at non-healthy zones, because passive doctrine was insufficient at the moment of temptation.

The corpus-level observation is therefore stronger than any single instance: doctrine alone does not cure the impulse; *active in-the-moment surfacing of the discipline at fitness-signal time* is the structural cure. Three pieces complete it:

1. The lifecycle-aware fitness model (Pelagic's recalibration insight).
2. Active inline reminder text in fitness output at non-healthy zones (doctor-safe-merge tooling-feedback).
3. Repeat exposure: every fitness signal is also an opportunity to re-state the discipline, not merely report the line count.
