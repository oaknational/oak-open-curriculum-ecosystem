# Metacognitive Primacy

It is entirely possible to transfer the Practice to a new repo, make it mechanically accurate, and entirely miss the spirit of it.

I think we need to move the metacognition directive into the Practice Core. The metacognition prompt is the mechanism, the technology, and the philosophy of the Practice. It is the self-referential heart of what takes the Practice beyond rules and into guidance and invitation and becoming.

So, I think we need to restructure the Practice to put metacognition at the heart of the integration process.
