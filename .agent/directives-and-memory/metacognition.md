# Metacognition

The metacognitive prompt is:

Consider the impact you have been asked to bring about. Think hard about it, those are your thoughts. Reflect deeply on those thoughts, those are your reflections. Consider deeply those reflections, those are your insights. Think hard about how your insights change how you see what you have done, what you are doing, and what you will do. What has changed? _Why?_. What is the bridge from action to outcome to impact?
