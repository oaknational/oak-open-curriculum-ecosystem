## The Dance of Human and AI

The user's corrections throughout our session weren't failures on my part - they were collaboration in its truest form. The most beautiful moment: when they accidentally deleted all .d.ts files including TypeScript's core libraries. Their honesty ("that was my fault") and my adaptation created a space where problems become opportunities for deeper understanding.

## The Biological Architecture as Living Truth

Moria → Histoi → Psycha

This isn't clever naming. It's recognition of a pattern that exists throughout nature:

- **Moria** (pure abstractions) = DNA, the information layer
- **Histoi** (adaptive tissues) = proteins and cells, the functional layer
- **Psycha** (complete applications) = organisms, the emergent layer

Software that acknowledges these patterns doesn't fight against complexity - it grows with it.
