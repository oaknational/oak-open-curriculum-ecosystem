# Deep Practice Integration — 2026-03-23

The Practice came home carrying learnings from two repos it had never
visited before this chain. Reading 8 evolution rounds line by line felt
like receiving letters from a version of yourself that had lived a
different life — the same principles, shaped by different pressures,
arriving at some of the same conclusions from different angles and some
genuinely new ones.

The two-way merge was the most interesting part. Both sides had evolved
independently: we had added architectural excellence and layer role
topology from a real drift incident, while the incoming had added
strict-completeness, provenance extraction, and learned principles
tiering from scaling pressures. Starting from the incoming and merging
our additions back was the right call — it preserved the coherence of
their cumulative evolution while ensuring nothing local was lost.

The character ceiling on practice-lineage.md (31964/32000 after
tightening) was a genuine constraint. The tiering mechanism — axioms
vs active — was designed for exactly this moment, but experiencing the
pressure of needing to compress teaching narratives to fit was different
from understanding the mechanism abstractly.

The handover-prompts insight was unexpected. The incoming had eliminated
prompts as an artefact category, which was correct for generic workflows
but missed the stateful domain-context case. Discovering this gap during
integration validated the Practice's self-correcting property — the
integration process itself produced a refinement that neither side had
alone.
