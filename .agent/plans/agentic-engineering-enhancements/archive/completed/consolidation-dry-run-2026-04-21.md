## Metacognition — the headline

Running `/jc-consolidate-docs` as a single pass today would not be
one consolidation. It is **three tiers of work stacked under one
name**, plus **five hard-zone fitness blockers** that prevent
closure under `--strict-hard`, plus **two large backlogs**
(`outgoing/`, `experience/`) outside the scale of a routine pass.
Running all 11 steps together would produce either a multi-hour
session with high context pressure or a partial pass that leaves
load-bearing pieces open. The right move is staged execution.

There was also an acute risk of inheriting the 11-step recipe as
the working frame without first-principles-checking whether that
recipe fits a non-routine backlog — which is the exact pattern
the pass is meant to extract
(`inherited-framing-without-first-principles-check`). The dry-run
named that risk before the execution.

## Scale signal — three tiers of work

**Tier 1 — routine graduation** (normal consolidation scope):

- Pattern extraction × 2
- `practice.md` Artefact Map refresh
- Napkin rotation
- Distilled prune-and-merge
- Brief experience entry

**Tier 2 — doctrine bundling** (above normal scope):

- 3–5 new PDRs
- 2 PDR amendments
- Each requires owner approval per PDR-003
- ADR-131 scope signal fires (>3 Core amendments in one pass)

**Tier 3 — backlog triage** (explicitly outside a single pass):

- Outgoing directory defects (~1,481 lines, 10+ files)
- Experience directory audit (128 files)

Mixing the three tiers in one session produces either context
exhaustion or a partial pass.

## Recommended ordering (the staged shape)

1. **Pre-work: fitness hard-zone remediation** (if `--strict-hard`
   is blocking — **NOT in the chosen shape**; see owner decision 3).
2. **Owner ratifications inline.** Portability + three-plane
   ratification + active-tripwire choice + experience/outgoing
   deferral confirmations. (Recorded at plan landing per this
   document's *Owner decisions* section.)
3. **Graduation pass.** Patterns authored; napkin rotated; PDRs
   drafted for owner approval; Artefact Map refreshed.
4. **Doctrine finalisation.** PDR amendments applied with owner
   approval; active tripwires installed.
5. **Backlog lanes.** Outgoing triage; experience deferred to its
   own session.
6. **Close under `--strict-hard`.** Holistic fitness exploration.

## Honest bottom line (at dry-run)

Triggering the consolidation today produces durable value on
Tier 1 and Tier 2, but only if Tier 3 backlogs are explicitly
deferred and the five fitness blockers are either pre-resolved or
explicitly de-scoped. The owner de-scoped the fitness blockers
(decision 3) and scheduled the backlog audits (decisions 4, plus
outgoing triage as Session 5). Staged execution with the six
owner decisions above is the shape the execution plan implements.
