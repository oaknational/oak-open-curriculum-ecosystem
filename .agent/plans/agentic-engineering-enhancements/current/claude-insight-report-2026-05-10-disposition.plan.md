### Root cause this plan addresses

Without an explicit disposition record, each future insight-report regeneration can re-pose the same items as fresh. The ledger is the structural cure: every item carries a documented decision once.

### Existing capabilities

- The graduation pipeline (napkin → distilled → ADR/PDR/pattern) already exists and is the right route for genuinely new content.
- `pending-graduations.md` already exists at `.agent/memory/operational/pending-graduations.md` and is the standard surface for items awaiting validation.
- The metacognition skill at `.agent/skills/metacognition/SKILL-CANONICAL.md` plus the `.agent/directives/metacognition.md` directive is the natural canonical home for drift-trigger material.

## Notes

### Why this matters (system-level thinking)

**Immediate value**:

- The integration discipline matches the user's documented preference (mining the report for canonical-surface input rather than treating it as a new surface).
- The disposition ledger is the structural record that prevents the same items being re-posed every time the report is regenerated.
- The discard rationale closes the loop on the `feedback_no_moving_targets_in_permanent_docs` discipline by making each "did not lift this" decision visible.

**System-level impact**:

- Future automated insight-report regeneration becomes useful as a delta-detector rather than a full re-audit each time.
- The graduation pipeline (napkin → distilled → pattern → directive) absorbs the small number of genuinely useful items without inflating with observations.
- The `pending-graduations.md` becomes an accurate account of what is pending, supporting the learning-loop tightening work.

**Risk of not doing**:

- Each future regeneration re-poses the same items, costing review time.
- Drift toward "lift everything for thoroughness" if the disposition discipline is not made structural.
- Loss of visibility into which observations have been considered and rejected versus genuinely new.

### First-Question Audit

> Could it be simpler without compromising quality?

Yes — and the simplification is the discard discipline itself. A naive "apply all 30" plan would inflate to 30 cycles of work that mostly restate existing canonical or lift moving targets. The honest plan applies what is net-new (1–2 items), routes the borderline (7 items) to the existing pending-graduations surface, and documents the discard rationale (20 items) once. The total integration footprint is one new pattern file plus one batch entry.
