## Atomic Concepts

Foundation concepts from which to build curriculum knowledge.

## Knowledge Graph and Ontology

A graph of curriculum entities and their relationships, including real content. This will build on atomic concepts. We already have the ability to explore our content semantically, the knowledge graph additionally enables discovery to happen by traversing the graph to related pedagogical concepts. The combination of these two capabilities is a game changing capability for the sector, and will have huge impact.

## Oak Open Curriculum API and Bulk Downloads

The API that surfaces our high-quality curriculum content to the world. The bulk downloads are a way to download the entire curriculum content in a machine readable format.

## SDKs and CLIs

SDKs and CLIs to lower the cost of using the Oak Open Curriculum API to near zero for external consumers. This is a primary driver of the value proposition of the Oak Open Curriculum in relations to the UK education sector, global education quality, ed-tech, and AI supporting education outcomes.

## Hybrid Semantic Search

Hybrid semantic search is a powerful way to search and discover content in the Oak Open Curriculum. It combines traditional keyword search with semantic search to find the most relevant content, across lessons, units and _sequences_.

## MCP tooling and AI apps

MCP tooling and AI apps will be built on top of the SDKs, enabling us and the sector to build new, safe, pedagogically rigorous education tools and resources, including AI agents and other AI applications.
