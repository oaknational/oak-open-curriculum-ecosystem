# Metacognition

**Governance**: This skill operationalises the first-principles
metacognition prompt ratified in
PDR-029 and the rush-impulse structural cue adopted in ADR-172.
The directive itself lives at
[`../../directives/metacognition.md`](../../directives/metacognition.md).

## Workflow

1. Read [`../../directives/metacognition.md`](../../directives/metacognition.md)
   end to end.
2. Apply it to the work in front of you, not as a summary exercise but as a
   pre-action or boundary reflection. Ask:
   - What did I inherit here?
   - Has the inherited shape been ratified from first principles?
   - Does the shape still fit the impact the user asked for?
   - What is the bridge from my next action to that impact?
3. Let the reflection change the work shape when it should. The output may be
   a revised plan, a narrower scope, a surfaced assumption, a deferral with
   evidence, or an explicit "no change" verdict with the reason.
4. If the pass produces only a recap of steps already taken, treat that as a
   failed metacognition pass. Re-read the directive and repeat until it yields
   an insight, a checked assumption, or a justified no-change verdict.
